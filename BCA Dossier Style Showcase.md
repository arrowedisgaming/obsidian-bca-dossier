## Typography Stress Test

This paragraph tests body rhythm at normal length. Special Elite carries the typewriter feel, but it's denser than a standard sans, so line-height is set to 1.6 to keep things readable across longer reading sessions. The reading column is capped at 720px — roughly matches a single column of a dossier page. **Bold hits in Oswald.** *Italic shifts to Atkinson.* Mix them together and you get **_strong emphasis in bold italic_** which reads as Oswald italic.

Inline variations: `mono code`, ==redacted==, **bold**, *italic*, ***bold italic***, ~~struck through~~, [a link](https://example.com), and [[an internal link]].
